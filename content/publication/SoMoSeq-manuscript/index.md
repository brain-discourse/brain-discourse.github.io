abstract: SoMoSeq (Somatic Mosaicism Sequencing) is a multi-omic single-nucleus method that pairs targeted DNA genotyping with full-length cDNA sequencing to link somatic variant status directly to transcriptional state in the same nucleus. Applied to mosaic brain tissue from individuals with hemimegalencephaly (PIK3CA) and focal cortical dysplasia type I (SLC35A2), the method resolves cell-type-specific variant burden and mutation-associated transcriptional signatures, including mTOR pathway upregulation, while confirming the early developmental origin of pathogenic somatic variants across neuronal and non-neuronal lineages. This manuscript is in preparation.
